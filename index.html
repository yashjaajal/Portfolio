004 — How I Think

The process
behind the output.

Step 01 — Diagnose
Start with the real problem.

Not the brief. Not the stated challenge. I dig into the brand, the category, the audience, and the competitive landscape before forming a single opinion. Most marketing problems are misdiagnosed — and strategies built on wrong diagnoses always fail.

Step 02 — Reframe
Find the cultural angle.

Every brand exists inside a culture. The best strategies find the tension, the truth, or the aspiration that makes a brand relevant beyond its product category. I look for the idea that makes the brand feel inevitable — not just logical.

Step 03 — Architect
Build the system, not just the campaign.

Strategy without structure is just opinion. I build frameworks — messaging hierarchies, content architectures, campaign systems, media models — that give the strategy a skeleton it can grow on. Everything is designed to scale.

Step 04 — Execute & Compound
Make the marketing compound.

The goal is never a one-off. Every campaign should build on the last — brand equity stacking with performance data, each cycle generating more signal. I track, learn, refine, and use the intelligence of previous work to make the next round smarter.
